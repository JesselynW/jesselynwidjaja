BerBISIk

PHP, SQL, Blade, HTML, CSS, JS, Figma

BerBISIk is an innovative platform designed to learn Bisindo (Indonesian Sign Language) more accessible to everyone. Through the website, users can apply to attend Bisindo courses. Additionally, BerBISIk features a comprehensive dictionary, allowing users to learn Bisindo signs at their own pace. The platform also offers opportunities to volunteer and contribute to the Bisindo community, helping to promote inclusivity and support for the Deaf and hard-of-hearing community in Indonesia.

Polluguard is a mobile app that allows users to monitor air quality in real-time while encouraging them to take action in their community. It provides updates on local air pollution levels, helps users understand the environmental impact, and offers volunteering opportunities for those who wish to contribute to cleaner air initiatives. Polluguard empowers users to be part of the solution by staying informed and actively participating in air quality improvement.

Calm

Figma

Calm is a platform dedicated to promoting mental health. Through the website, users can access a wealth of information on mental health, learn about various conditions, and find tips for managing stress and anxiety. Calm also connects users with licensed doctors for online consultations, making mental health support more accessible and personalized. The goal is to offer a safe space where individuals can find guidance, resources, and professional help when they need it.
